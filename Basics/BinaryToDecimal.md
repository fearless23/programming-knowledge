# Binary to Decimal

## How to convert binary to integer

### number=1001
- (1 * 2<sup>3</sup>) + (0 * 2<sup>2</sup>)+ (0 * 2<sup>1</sup>) + 1 = 9
- or (1 * 8) + (0 * 4) + (0 * 2) + 1 = 9

### fraction= .1001
- (1 * (1/2)<sup>1</sup>) + (0 * (1/2)<sup>2</sup>)+ (0 * (1/2)<sup>3</sup>) + (1 * * (1/2)<sup>4</sup>) = 9
- or (1/2) + (0/4) + (0/8) + (1/16) = 9/16 = 0.5625
